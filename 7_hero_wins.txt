Every encounter with any of the dangerous monsters he wins, and after a while he can see
the shining treasure from a far distance. Suddenly The Vengeful Ox appears, as he also is
looking for the very same treasure. Both of them drew their weapons and started fighting
and after several days of fighting Mighty Mamba wins.
But Mamba only won because of Bongo the giraffe, who had mistakingly poisoned The Vengeful Ox.
